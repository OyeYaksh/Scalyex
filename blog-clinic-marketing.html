Clinic Marketing in Gurugram: 5 Things That Actually Work

July 2026 · 6 min read · By Yaksh, Scalyex

Agar aapki clinic mein patients acchi service ke baad bhi kam aa rahe hain, problem aapki expertise nahi — aapki visibility hai. Gurugram mein har week nayi clinics khul rahi hain. Google par dikhna ab optional nahi, zaroori hai.

1. Google Business Profile optimise karo

"Physiotherapy near me" ya "dental clinic Sector 56" — ye searches Google Maps se start hote hain. Complete profile, regular photos, review responses, aur sahi categories lagao. Local SEO ka sabse fast ROI yahi se aata hai.

2. Educational reels, promotional nahi

Patients trust doctors jo explain karte hain — sell nahi karte. 30-second reels: "Back pain ke 3 common causes", "Post-surgery physio kab shuru karein". Humne Healthy Being ke saath ye approach use ki — engagement 85% badha.

3. Reviews systematically collect karo

Har satisfied patient se WhatsApp par review link bhejo. 20+ genuine Google reviews = local ranking mein massive difference.

4. Consistent posting > viral posting

Week mein 3 quality posts beat karte hain random viral attempts ko. Calendar banao, templates use karo, consistency build karo.

5. Local Meta ads with appointment CTA

₹500–800/day budget se aap 5km radius target kar sakte ho. Creative mein doctor ka face, clinic address, aur "Book appointment" CTA — simple works.
