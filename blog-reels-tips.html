Reels Ke Liye 7 Hook Formulas Jo Actually Kaam Karti Hain

July 2026 · 5 min read · By Yaksh, Scalyex

Reel ka pehla 2 second decide karta hai — scroll ya stop. Hook weak hai toh editing kitni bhi acchi ho, views nahi aayenge. Yahan 7 formulas hain jo hum apne clients ke liye use karte hain:

  1. "Galat hai jo aap soch rahe ho..." — myth busting. Example: "Protein sirf gym jaane walon ke liye nahi hai."
  2. "3 cheezein jo main wish karta..." — list format, fast cuts.
  3. "Maine ye galti ki aur..." — vulnerability + lesson. High saves.
  4. "₹X budget mein kaise..." — D2C brands ke liye perfect.
  5. "POV: Aap [customer] ho..." — clinic/ service businesses ke liye.
  6. "Before vs After (real)" — social proof, no fake claims.
  7. "Kya aap bhi ye sign ignore kar rahe ho?" — health/wellness hooks.

Pro tip: Hook likho pehle, phir video shoot karo. 80% reels fail isliye hoti hain kyunki pehle shoot, baad mein sochna.
