Dr. Arpita's physiotherapy center had the clinical excellence but lacked the digital visibility to attract the patients they deserved. Scalyex stepped in to build their digital presence from scratch — from brand positioning to content creation and execution.

Brand Consistency

The Challenge

No consistent social media presence. No brand identity across platforms. Patient acquisition relying entirely on word-of-mouth with zero digital footprint.

Our Approach

Built a consistent brand voice and visual identity. Established a monthly content calendar with educational reels, patient trust content, and service spotlights. Deployed and managed across Instagram.

The Outcome

Tripled organic reach within 3 months. Engagement rate increased significantly. The clinic now has a professional, trustworthy digital presence that actively works for them.
