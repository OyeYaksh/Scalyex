Frequently Asked Questions.

Everything you need to know before we work together.

General

Scalyex is a growth-focused marketing agency based in Gurugram. We help brands scale through data-driven strategies, high-converting content, and performance-led execution. Our core services include Digital Marketing Strategy, Social Media Management, Content & Reels, Performance Ads, Branding, and Website Design.

We work with growth-stage businesses, founders, clinics, personal brands, and companies that want to build a serious digital presence. Whether you're starting from scratch or optimising an existing strategy, we tailor our approach to your specific stage and goals.

Services

Process

Start by booking a free 30-minute strategy call or filling out the contact form. We'll learn about your brand, goals, and current challenges. Then we'll send you a tailored proposal outlining our recommended approach, deliverables, and pricing.

Organic growth (social media, content) typically shows meaningful traction in 60–90 days. Paid campaigns can show results in the first 2–4 weeks with proper testing. We set clear KPI benchmarks at the start so you always know what to expect and when.

We provide weekly updates and a full monthly performance report. You'll have a dedicated communication channel (WhatsApp or email) for day-to-day questions. We believe in proactive communication — you'll never be left wondering what's happening.

Pricing

Both. Our core services run on monthly retainers for consistent, compounding results. One-time projects (website design, brand identity) are quoted separately. We'll recommend the right structure based on your needs during the strategy call.
