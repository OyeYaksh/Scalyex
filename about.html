The Origin

Why Scalyex Exists

Yaksh started Scalyex after seeing too many good businesses fail online — not because their product was bad, but because their marketing was generic, inconsistent, and disconnected from real business goals.

The agency was built on a simple idea: treat every client's brand like it's your own. No templates, no automation without strategy, no reports that look good but mean nothing.

Today, Scalyex operates as a lean, founder-led team focused on delivering clarity and consistent growth for clinics, D2C brands, and ambitious founders across India.
